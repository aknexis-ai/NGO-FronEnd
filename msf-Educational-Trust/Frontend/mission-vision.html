Mission & Vision

Mission

MSF Educational Trust provides a sensitive and caring environment for street and working children, and those in difficult circumstances. We seek to create a platform where children can realise their full potential ‚Äî ensuring their right to a safe and secure space, education, health, and nutrition.

Learn More

Vision

MSF Educational Trust aims to create a nurturing environment that fosters the physical, emotional, and mental growth of street and working children. Our vision is to help children experience a safe childhood and turn their dreams into reality.

Learn More

MSF Educational Trust is dedicated to empowering underprivileged children and communities through education, care, and life-building opportunities. Together, we build hope, dignity, and a brighter future.
