24√ó7 Child Help Desk

Our 24√ó7 Child Help Desk serves as a constant support system for children in need, ensuring immediate assistance, protection, and guidance at any hour of the day.

Our Mission and Objectives

The mission of the 24√ó7 Child Help Desk is to provide timely intervention and holistic support to children facing distress, abuse, neglect, or exploitation. We strive to create a safe and responsive system that prioritizes the rights, dignity, and well-being of every child.

  • Ensure immediate response to children in crisis
  • Provide emotional, legal, and social support
  • Safeguard child rights and protection
  • Facilitate access to care, shelter, and rehabilitation

Our Approach

Our approach is child-centric, confidential, and action-oriented. Every call, report, or referral is handled with sensitivity, urgency, and professionalism. Trained staff assess the situation and coordinate appropriate interventions in collaboration with relevant authorities and support systems.

  • 24√ó7 availability with trained personnel
  • Confidential and child-friendly response mechanism
  • Immediate assessment and action planning
  • Coordination with rescue, shelter, and rehabilitation services

Stakeholder Engagement

Effective child protection requires strong collaboration. The 24√ó7 Child Help Desk works closely with multiple stakeholders to ensure comprehensive and coordinated responses for every child in need.

  • Collaboration with Child Welfare Committees (CWC)
  • Coordination with police and legal authorities
  • Partnerships with NGOs and shelter homes
  • Engagement with schools and community leaders
  • Referral networks for healthcare and counseling services

MSF Educational Trust is dedicated to empowering underprivileged children and communities through education, care, and life-building opportunities. Together, we build hope, dignity, and a brighter future.
