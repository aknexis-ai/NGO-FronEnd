Full Care Residential Programme

Full Care Residential Programme

MSF Educational Trust operates full care residential centres and shelter homes that provide a safe, nurturing, and structured environment for children in need of care and protection.

Our residential programmes focus on holistic development ‚Äî ensuring access to education, healthcare, nutrition, emotional well-being, life skills, and reintegration into society wherever possible.

Programme Overview

The Full Care Residential Programme is designed for children who are homeless, abandoned, orphaned, or at risk. These children are provided with a secure living space, continuous care, and professional support systems that help them rebuild their lives with dignity and hope.

Each residential centre operates in compliance with child protection laws and is staffed by trained caregivers, educators, counselors, and healthcare professionals.

Aasra Children Home for Boys (CHB)

Najafgarh

Aasra Children Home for Boys provides long-term residential care for boys who have faced neglect, abuse, or homelessness. The centre focuses on education, discipline, emotional healing, and character development.

Boys are supported through formal schooling, vocational training, counseling, and recreational activities that encourage confidence and independence.

DMRC Children Home for Boys (CHB)

Tis Hazari

The DMRC Children Home for Boys serves children rescued from vulnerable situations, offering immediate care and long-term rehabilitation services.

The centre ensures access to education, healthcare, nutrition, and structured daily routines that promote stability and growth.

Aarushi Children Home for Girls (CHG)

Gurugram

Aarushi Children Home for Girls provides a safe and empowering space for girls in need of care and protection. The centre emphasizes education, health, self-esteem, and life skills.

Special attention is given to emotional well-being, counseling, and preparing girls for independent and confident futures.

DMRC Udaan Children Home for Girls (CHG)

Dilshad Garden

DMRC Udaan Children Home for Girls supports girls who have experienced difficult life circumstances. The programme focuses on healing, education, and personal growth.

Through structured routines and compassionate care, the centre helps girls rebuild confidence and pursue their aspirations.

Apna Ghar Open Shelter (OS)

Old Delhi Railway Station

Apna Ghar Open Shelter provides immediate, short-term care and protection for children found in vulnerable conditions around the Old Delhi Railway Station.

The shelter acts as a first point of contact, offering food, rest, medical assistance, counseling, and referral services.

ODRS Open Shelter (OS)

Old Delhi Railway Station

The ODRS Open Shelter focuses on outreach, protection, and rehabilitation of children found in and around railway premises.

The programme ensures immediate safety, counseling, and coordination with child welfare authorities for long-term care solutions.

MSF Educational Trust is dedicated to empowering underprivileged children and communities through education, care, and life-building opportunities. Together, we build hope, dignity, and a brighter future.
