Mental Health

Mental Health Program

Our Mental Health Program is designed to promote emotional well-being, psychological resilience, and access to professional mental health support for children, adolescents, and vulnerable communities.

Vision

To create a society where mental health is recognized as a fundamental component of overall well-being, and where every individual has access to compassionate, timely, and stigma-free mental health support.

Objectives

  • Promote awareness and understanding of mental health issues
  • Provide early identification and emotional support
  • Reduce stigma associated with mental health challenges
  • Ensure access to counseling and professional care
  • Strengthen coping skills and emotional resilience

Services Under Mental Health Program

Our services address mental health needs through a holistic, child-friendly, and community-based approach, ensuring confidentiality, care, and continuity of support.

  • Individual and group counseling sessions
  • Psychological first aid and crisis intervention
  • Referral to mental health professionals
  • Life skills and emotional well-being workshops
  • Parental guidance and caregiver counseling
